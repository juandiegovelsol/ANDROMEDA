Retro Equation Builder | Match the target to score points!

HOW TO PLAY

>

GOAL: Use the available tokens to build an equation that equals the TARGET number.

>

DRAG & DROP: Drag number and operator tokens from the palette on the right into the empty slots. In the equation, click on tokens to remove them.

>

VALIDATE: The game automatically checks if your equation is valid and if the result matches the target.

>

SCORE: Find a correct equation to score points and get a new target. The faster you are, the more points you get!

>

DIFFICULTY: Change the challenge by selecting Easy, Medium, or Hard. This will start a new game.

>

CONTROLS: Use the NEW GAME button to restart, or CLEAR ALL to empty the equation slots.
